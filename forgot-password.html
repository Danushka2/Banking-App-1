If you have forgotten your Sampath Vishwa password, please follow these steps to reset it and regain access.

  1. Contact your nearest Sampath Bank branch and request to reset your password. The bank will reset both Login and Transaction passwords for security reasons.
  2. You will receive a notification via e-mail informing you that the password is reset, to your registered e-mail.
  3. Visit www.bankname.com and enter your user name in the user name text box and click sign in. Leave the password text box blank at this time.
  4. You can enter new login and transaction passwords on the screen provided now. However, for the security reasons your access is suspended till you complete the documentation.
  5. Download Acknowledgment form by clicking the link (Download Acknowledgement Form) on the screen, sign it and either post or hand it over to any bank branch of your convenience.
